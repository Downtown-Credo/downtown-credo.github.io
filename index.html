Our Location

North Quarter

Our first co-location, this shop is in the North Quarter of Orlando at the base of the NORA apartment complex. We share the space with indie bookstore Zeppelin Books and host a rotating exhibition of local art curated by Artscape. In addition to coffee and tea, we now serve light bites and full meals from our vegan kitchen, featuring toasts, sandwiches, and salads as well as a brunch menu! Open six days a week, it's a great spot to start your day, take a lunch break, or peruse for your next favorite book.
