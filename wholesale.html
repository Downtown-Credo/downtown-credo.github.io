Wholesale

Partner with Us We at Downtown CREDO are proud to partner with cafés, restaurants, offices and organizations who actively pursue meaningful impact in the context of their communities and desire to achieve excellence in their craft. We strive to partner with food and beverage service industry partners to serve specialty coffee, the best consulting and training, and the right equipment to make the business a success.

Bulk Coffee

We offer wholesale pricing of all of our coffees in 5 lb bags, whole bean or ground.

Packaged Retail

Featuring our Foundational single-origin coffees, carefully crafted blends, and select small batch boxes, our retail program is sure to elevate your coffee program.

Cold Brew

The “407” blend we crafted for our cold brew drips with notes of molasses, mandarin oranges, and peanut butter cups. Sold as concentrate or ready-to-serve in 5 gallon kegs, 2.5 gallon kegs, or 64 oz jugs.

Consulting

We have years of experience in coffee business consulting; we would love to come alongside you as a wholesale partner to help shape or reshape your concept. Our seasoned team can assist with all of the details of running a successful coffee business.

Equipment

We are able to help you find coffee equipment from choice manufacturers and will work with your budget to build the package that best suits your vision.

Training

Our training consists of everything from extensive coffee knowledge to espresso bar workflow. Inquire below for more information!

Wholesale Inquiry

We got into coffee philosophically, but we also love great coffee. Smart roasting is a key step in great coffee. Our roasting happens in small batches right in downtown Orlando so you'll always be drinking coffee at its peak freshness. Our aim is for the roast to draw out the substantial flavors of our carefully selected beans, so you can enjoy all the flavors that our growers work so hard to produce.

For select wholesale partners, we're ready to offer our Honduran and Guatemalan beans.All CREDO coffee is premium, single origin coffee roasted locally. Prices vary with volume but starts at a minimum of 5lbs per month. Cups, stirrers, and other fixings available upon request.
